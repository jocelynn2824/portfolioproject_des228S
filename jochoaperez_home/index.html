Hi, I’m Jocelyn!

I’m a senior Visual Communication Design major at San Francisco State University. As a first-generation Mexican–Salvadoran American, I bring a deep sense of cultural pride to my work.

I’m passionate about using design for representation, accessibility, and clear communication. I believe design should not only look good, but also be easy to understand and inclusive to everyone.

Though I’m still early in my journey, I focus on purpose-driven work—across layout, typography, and illustration. My projects are rooted in meaning, inspired by films, literature, concerts, and everyday life.

I’d love to connect with those who value simplicity, clarity, and cultural storytelling in visual design.
